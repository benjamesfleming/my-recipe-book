### Ingredients:

- 3 tbsp Almond Meal
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tsp Baking Power
- 1 tbsp Melted Butter

### Steps:

1. Mix all ingredients in a microwave safe bowl.
2. Microwave on high for 90 seconds.
3. Enjoy.
